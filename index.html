BC Hydro's Lower Mainland Softball League

With spring almost here and weather slowly improving, now is a great time to consider your outdoor activities. There is no better time to enjoy a spring and summer weeknight than outside playing the game of baseball with friends. Whether you want to create your own team, or join an existing team individually (or with others), please check out the league details below and reach out to us with your interest.

This is a fun league as we encourage players of all levels to come out and play. We have players who have been in the league for many years, but we're always on the lookout for fresh talent and new friends. Friday, April 19 is the deadline to submit your team, or your interest in joining an existing team.

20+ Years of League History

The league been has been around for more than 20 years! Every team is a mixture of employee players from BC Hydro and Powerex, as well as immediate family members. Over the years we have also had teams from BC Gas and BCTC.

Eligibility

All current and retired employees of BC Hydro, BC Hydro's contractors, Powerex, Powertech, and TELUS and their immediate family members are eligible to play. All players must be at least 17 years of age due to insurance requirements.

Recruitment

The BC Hydro Slow Pitch League is continuously looking for teams and/or individual players interested in joining the league and currently have 2 available team spots for this upcoming season to add to the existing 10 teams we have in place for the upcoming season.

Season/Game Details

  • The league's season runs from May to August each year. The first game is on Wednesday, May 1 for the 2019 season

  • Games are played on Wednesday nights at Gordon Park in Vancouver (East 49th Avenue and Argyle Street)

  • Game times are 5:45 pm and 7:05 pm and are 1 hour and 15 minutes in length

  • The league is co-ed with 10 fielders playing at one time (includes a minimum of 3 women)

  • Teams can bat an unlimited number of players

  • Team fees are approximately $500, however, individual fees for players are determined by the team captains

Year End Tournament

  • The league's year end tournament is usually scheduled for the weekend of August 24 - 25, and is played at Moody Park in New Westminster

  • To be eligible for the year end tournament, an individual is required to play a minimum of 4 games during the regular season

Join Us!

If you are interested in creating your own team, or joining an existing team, please send an email to bchslowpitch@hotmail.com by Friday, April 19.

This is a great opportunity for employees and their families from all these companies to socialize and have some fun. Feel free to check out our website from 2018.
